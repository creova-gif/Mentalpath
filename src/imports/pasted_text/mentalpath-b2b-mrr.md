6-week MVP build plan
MVP: Solo practitioner only, core 6 features, beta with your existing client
Wk 1–2
Auth + practitioner setup: Therapist profile (credentials, specialization including cultural focus, province registration number). PHIPA data residency confirmed (AWS Canada-Central region or Supabase Canada).
Wk 2–3
Client management + intake: Client records, intake form builder (pre-built culturally-adapted templates: newcomer trauma, racialized stress, cultural humility framework). Consent forms with e-sign.
Wk 3–4
Booking + calendar: Therapist availability settings, client self-booking link, automated reminders (SMS/email), Google Calendar sync, session duration + type (individual, couples, family).
Wk 4–5
Notes + billing: SOAP / DAP / progress note templates, secure per-client note storage. Stripe-based payment on booking, automatic receipt generation, sliding scale rate setting, invoice history.
Wk 5–6
Beta launch: Your existing psychotherapist client goes live as beta user. Gather 2 weeks of real feedback. Onboard 5 more solo practitioners from BIPOC therapist networks (CAMH, TAIBU, Black mental health Canada).
Go-to-market — community channel strategy
—
Your existing client is your case study. Get permission to use their practice as a testimonial. One real therapist saying "I switched from TherapyNotes and this is PHIPA-compliant for $49/mo" is worth $10,000 in ads.
—
Target culturally-specific therapist communities: Black mental health practitioners network, CAMH cultural programs, South Asian mental health practitioners, TAIBU Community Health Centre. These practitioners are actively looking for tools that understand their client base.
—
Compliance migration campaign: Run content specifically targeting practitioners using TherapyNotes/TheraNest. "Your US therapy software is not PHIPA compliant — here's what that means and how to switch." This is a pain-point-first GTM with urgency.
—
College partnerships: Ryerson/TMU, York, U of T — all have MSW and psychotherapy programs. Offer a student discount ($9/mo) to graduating students who are setting up practices. They become lifetime customers.
