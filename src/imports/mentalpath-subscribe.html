Common questions
When will I be charged? +
Not until your 30-day free trial ends on April 15, 2026. You can cancel before then and you won't pay anything. After the trial, billing is monthly and you can cancel any time from Settings → Billing.
Is my client data safe? +
All client health records are stored exclusively on Canadian servers (AWS ca-central-1, Montreal and Toronto). Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). MentalPath is designed to meet PHIPA requirements. Your billing data is handled by Stripe — it never touches MentalPath servers.
Can I switch plans? +
Yes — you can upgrade from Solo to Group at any time from Settings. Downgrading is available at renewal. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you're billed the prorated difference.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your account is paused, not deleted. You retain read-only access to your records for 90 days. Clinical records are retained for 10 years as required by PHIPA regardless of subscription status — you'll receive a full data export before any deletion.
Do you offer a community discount? +
Yes — 20% discount for therapists who primarily serve BIPOC, newcomer, or refugee communities, and for therapists in rural/remote areas. Email hello@mentalpath.ca with a brief description of your practice.
